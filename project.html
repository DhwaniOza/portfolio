Behind the Shot: Balancing Light and Texture

This image was part of my November 2024 portfolio assignment at Georgian College. The goal was to experiment with light, textures, and composition to create a visually compelling shot. I carefully arranged wooden chips, stones, and ice cubes on a wooden surface to complement the contrasting colors of the two smartphones.

Water droplets were added strategically to enhance the reflective texture of the devices, making them pop under controlled lighting. The subtle shadows and highlights create depth, while the background elements frame the main subjects effectively.

This project allowed me to explore product photography techniques, emphasizing attention to detail and lighting precision. It remains one of my favorite captures for its simplicity and powerful visual storytelling.

Celebrating Faith and Culture Through the Lens

This image was captured during the Ganesh Chaturthi celebrations held at Loyalist College, Belleville. The vibrant colors, intricate details of the idol, and the essence of devotion inspired me to preserve this moment through my lens.

The image highlights the cultural richness and the collective spirit of the community coming together to celebrate this auspicious festival. The soft lighting, natural background blur, and focus on the idol create a serene and intimate connection.

This project allowed me to explore **event photography** in a cultural setting, emphasizing storytelling through visuals. It stands as a reminder of how traditions and art blend seamlessly in moments of worship and celebration.

Whispering Shadows: A Short Horror Film

Description

"Whispering Shadows" is a spine-chilling short horror film that I, Dhwani Oza, directed as my final year project at Loyalist College. It was a thrilling and creatively enriching experience to write and direct this film, which explores the eerie whispers of unseen entities that plague a small family. The film's tension builds through a combination of atmospheric visuals and an original background score.

Collaborating with a talented team made this project incredibly rewarding. It was not only an opportunity to push creative boundaries but also a lesson in teamwork and problem-solving during high-pressure shoots. I fondly remember the countless hours we spent rehearsing scenes and adjusting lighting to get the perfect mood for every shot.

iPhone 15 Pro Max vs Samsung S24 Ultra

Project Overview

This project was part of an **assignment at Georgian College** in 2024. The goal was to create an engaging comparison video between two flagship devices: the **iPhone 15 Pro Max** and **Samsung S24 Ultra**.

Every aspect of the project, including **scripting, filming, and editing**, was managed solely by me. The video highlights differences in **camera performance, design, software features, and user experience** of both devices.

I used **Adobe Premiere Pro** for editing, ensuring seamless transitions, balanced audio levels, and engaging visual storytelling.
